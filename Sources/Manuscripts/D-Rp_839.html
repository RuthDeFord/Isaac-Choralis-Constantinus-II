Provenance: Copied in Regensburg, probably for the Gymnasium Poeticum, possibly by Erasmus Zollner. Dates 1571-73 in manuscript.

DIAMM entry

Source of piece in Choralis Constantinus II:

The readings in the Choralis piece are almost identical to those in RISM 15455 except for one fairly obvious error in the bass that is corrected in D-Rp A. R. 839. The piece must have been copied from RISM 15455 or a copy of it.
