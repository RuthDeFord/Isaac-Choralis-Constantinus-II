D-OBa Sign. Lit. 3

Ottobeuren, Bibliothek der Benediktiner Abtei, Sign. Lit. 3

Choirbook of liturgical music.

Provenance: Copied by Frater Christian Frantz for the Benedictine monastery in Ottobeuren, 1577.

Published inventory and study: Johannes Hoyer, "Frater Christian Frantz' Chorbuch von 1577 (Sign. Lit. 3) aus der Benediktinerabtei Ottobeuren," Neues musikwissenschaftliches Jahrbuch 10 (2001): 15-70. Some of the Isaac pieces are identified incorrectly.

No DIAMM entry.

Source of pieces in Choralis Constantinus II:

Probably copied from the Choralis print.

Choralis Constantinus II contents:

  • Mass 1
    • Introit-Puer natus est (fol. 2r) - verse, A and B only
  • Mass 5
    • Introit-Rorate coeli (fol. 16v)
  • Mass 8
    • Introit-Spiritus Domini (fol. 44v)
  • Mass 9
    • Alleluia-Caro mea (fol. 85v) - for Corpus Christi and Translation of S. Alexander, with different text
  • Mass 15
    • Alleluia-Assumpta est Maria (fol. 68v)
  • Mass 22
    • Alleluia-Martinus episcopus (fol. 80v)
  • Mass 23
    • Alleluia-Felix virgo (fol. 81v)
Character of variants in Choralis Constantinus II pieces:

Two pieces (Introit 8 and Alleluia 9) have substantial cuts that must be intentional, because they correspond in all voices.

Introit 8 has a revised version of a decorative melisma in which a pair of fusae is replaced by a semiminim.

One piece has an alternative liturgical assignment and text.
