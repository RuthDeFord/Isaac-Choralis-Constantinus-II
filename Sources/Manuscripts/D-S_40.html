D-Stuttg MS Musica folio I 40

Stuttgart, Württembergische Landesbibliothek, MS Musica folio I 40

Choirbook of liturgical music

Provenance: Copied by Nikolaus Peuschel in Stuttgart, for use by court chapel of Ulrich, Duke of Württemberg. Before 1550.

Published inventory: Clytus Gottwald. Codices Musici (Cod. Mus. Fol. I 1-71). Die Handschriften der Württembergischen Landesbibliothek Stuttgart. Wiesbaden, 1964. Pages 70-72.

DIAMM entry

Source of piece in Choralis Constantinus II: Probably transmitted from Constance by Sixt Dietrich.

Choralis Constantinus II contents:

  • Mass 9
    • Introit-Cibavit eos (fol. 125v)
    • Alleluia-Caro mea (fol. 129v)
    • Sequence-Lauda Sion (fol. 135v)
