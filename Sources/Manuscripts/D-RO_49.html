DIAMM entry

Sources of pieces in Choralis Constantinus II:

Introits 1 and 3 must have been copied from RISM 15455 or a copy of it. Introit 1 joins Isaac's verse with a different antiphon from RISM 15455, and the readings of Introit 3 are nearly identical to those in RISM 15455. Introit 13 was copied from a copy of the printer's exemplar.

Choralis Constantinus II contents:

  • Mass 1
    • Introit-Puer natus (verse only; joined to antiphon by Sixt Dietrich from RISM 15455 (vol. I, Introit no. 3)
  • Mass 3
    • Introit-Ecce advenit (vol. I, Introit no. 5)
  • Mass 13
    • Introit-Gaudeamus omnes (vol. 1, Introit no. 18)

Character of variants in Choralis Constantinus II repertoire:

Introit 13 has a different liturgical assignment (Mary Magdalen instead of Visitation) and some textual variants to adapt it to its new liturgical role.
