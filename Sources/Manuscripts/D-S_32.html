D-Stuttg MS Musica folio I 32

Stuttgart, Württembergische Landesbibliothek, MS Musica folio I 32

Choirbook of liturgical music

Provenance: Copied by Nikolaus Peuschel in Stuttgart, for use by court chapel of Ulrich, Duke of Württemberg. Date 1543 in manuscript.

Published inventory: Clytus Gottwald. Codices Musici (Cod. Mus. Fol. I 1-71). Die Handschriften der Württembergischen Landesbibliothek Stuttgart. Wiesbaden, 1964. Pages 56-8.

DIAMM entry

Source of piece in Choralis Constantinus II: Probably transmitted from Constance by Sixt Dietrich.
