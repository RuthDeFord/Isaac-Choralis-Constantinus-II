DIAMM entry

Sources of pieces in Choralis Constantinus II:

Alleluia 1 shares distinctive readings with 15455 that prove that it must have been copied from that source or a copy of it.

The other six pieces form uninterrupted groups within each mass. The relations of their readings to the print and to other pre-print sources imply they were copied from the printer's exemplar or a copy of it.
