Officia de Nativitate, Circumcisione, Epiphania Domini, et de Purificatione beatae Mariae Virginis (Wittenberg: Georg Rhau, 1545).

Large collection of mass propers for the Nativity, Circumcision, Epiphany, and the Purification of the B.V.M. by various composers.

Character of variants in the Choralis Constantinus II repertoire: The pieces in Choralis Constaninus II display several distinctive errors and variants that distinguish them from pieces transmitted through Nuremberg to the Formschneider print. These readings make it possible to identify later copies based on 15455 in most cases.
